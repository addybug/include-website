FAQ

Is this program only for girls with experience programming?

No. Although the goal of this program is to provide support above the beginner level, every session has multiple options for different skill levels so we are beginner friendly! If you have any concerns about whether you are right for the program, send us an email.

What does the program cost?

The program is 100% free.

Do I have to go to every session?

No. We understand that everyone’s summer schedules are irregular. Our sessions are designed to stand on their own unless stated otherwise in the event description page. We encourage you to come to as many as you can, though.

Is this program only for middle school girls?

No. While this program is targeted towards middle school girls, if you are interested in participating but not in middle school, just shoot us an email to let us know. Everyone is welcome to join!

Do I need to have my own laptop?

No. We have some laptops that we will provide to anyone who needs them. Just indicate when you RSVP that you need a laptop so we can make sure we have enough.

Can I bring my own laptop?

Yes, that is prefered so laptops can go to those who need them. Just make sure you download all the requirements for that day’s session before arrival so we don’t have to spend time on that. If you need help downloading programs or libraries, send us an email and we can help you out!

What if I can’t get transportation to the session?

Transportation should never be a barrier for learning. If you cannot get to the session, send us an email and we will provide everything you need to do the lab.

How can I improve my skills?

Check out our resources page to find our favorite websites and free courses on a variety of different CS-related skills.

What do the donations go towards?

We use the donated money to go towards website hosting and purchasing laptops and tech supplies for our sessions. The program is not for-profit and every session is free. Thank you so much for your donations!

If I’m a high school girl and I’m passionate about computer science, can I help out with the sessions?

Yes! We’d love to have more members on our team. If you are interested in volunteering with us, send us an email!
